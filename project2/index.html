Final Project Proposal

This proposal outlines my plan and my goals for my Final Project website. The purpose of this proposal is to describe the purpose of the website, define its intended structure, and identify the resources I will use to design and build it.

1. Project Concept

Overview

For my final project, I intend to design and build a professional portfolio website that showcases my work, skills, and experience in web development and design. The website will present my projects in a clear, organized format that is easy to navigate and visually appealing to potential employers, clients, and collaborators.

Objectives and Goals

The primary goals of the website are to:

  1. Create a professional online presence that effectively showcases my technical skills and completed projects
  2. Provide clear navigation and organization that allows visitors to easily explore my work
  3. Offer multiple ways for potential employers or clients to learn about me and make contact

The intended audience for my website includes potential employers, clients, and professional contacts in the web development and technology fields.

Planned features for the website include:

  • Project showcase with descriptions and images
  • About page with professional background and skills
  • Responsive design that works on all devices
  • Contact information and email link

Note that some interactive features may be non-functional prototypes for the purpose of this class project.

2. Site Map

Website Organization

The website content will be organized into four primary pages. Navigation links will be available on all pages to ensure consistent and intuitive movement throughout the site.

Pages included on the website:

  • Home (index.html) - Welcome page with overview and featured projects
  • About - Professional background, skills, and experience
  • Projects - Portfolio of work with project descriptions and images
  • Contact - Contact information and email form

Layout and Navigation

The website will use a clean, professional layout with consistent design across all pages. There will be a header at the top of each page containing the site logo/name and the main navigation menu; a footer will be displayed at the bottom showing copyright information and additional navigation links. The main content area will be centered on the page with appropriate spacing and readable typography.

Homepage Design

The homepage wireframe includes a header with site navigation, a hero section with a brief introduction, a featured projects section showcasing selected work, and a footer with contact information.
